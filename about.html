Our Mission

Connecting surplus food with those who need it most. Together, we can end food waste.

Who We Are

We are a team of passionate individuals committed to addressing the issue of food waste in India. Our goal is to create a seamless ecosystem that connects food donors with charities and NGOs, while significantly reducing the environmental impact of food waste.

Our Vision

Compassion

Every donation is a step towards a more compassionate world where no one goes hungry.

Sustainability

Reducing waste isn't just about hunger; it's about protecting our planet for future generations.

Community

We believe in the power of collective action. When we work together, we achieve more.

Where We Project

F.W.M.S.

The Food Waste Management System connects donors with those in need. We collect excess food from hotels, halls, and restaurants to distribute it efficiently.
